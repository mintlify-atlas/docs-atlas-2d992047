HelloGitHub is powered by a vibrant community of developers, learners, and open-source enthusiasts. Every project featured in the monthly issues was discovered and submitted by a community member — someone who found something worth sharing and took the time to write it up. Since the project began in 2016, more than **458 contributors** have had at least one project accepted and published in an official issue.

## Core Contributors

Contributors who have had **10 or more projects accepted** earn core contributor status — a recognition of sustained, high-quality curation over time. Core contributors have a deep familiarity with what HelloGitHub looks for and consistently submit projects that resonate with the community.

## How to Become a Contributor

Anyone can submit a project — there is no application process or prior qualification required. The path from first submission to listed contributor is straightforward.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Submit a project via GitHub Issues">
    Open a new issue on the [HelloGitHub repository](https://github.com/521xueweihan/HelloGitHub/issues/new/choose) using the official submission template. Fill in the project URL, category, title, description, and highlights. Both English and Chinese submission forms are available.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Project gets reviewed and accepted">
    Maintainers review each submission against the project's acceptance criteria — originality, activity, beginner-friendliness, and quality of the description. You may be asked to revise your description before acceptance.
  </Step>
  <Step title="GitHub username listed in contributors.md">
    Once your project is accepted and published in a monthly issue, your GitHub username is permanently added to the [contributors list](https://github.com/521xueweihan/HelloGitHub/blob/master/content/contributors.md) on GitHub. You will also be notified directly in your submission issue.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reach 10+ accepted projects → core contributor status">
    Accumulate 10 or more accepted project submissions and you will be promoted to the core contributors section — the highest recognition the HelloGitHub community offers to curators.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Contributor Recognition

HelloGitHub takes care to acknowledge every contributor who has a project accepted. When your submission is approved and scheduled for inclusion in an upcoming issue, the maintainers will post a notification directly in the GitHub issue you opened for that submission — so you always know when your work has made it in.

Your GitHub username is then permanently listed in the [`contributors.md`](https://github.com/521xueweihan/HelloGitHub/blob/master/content/contributors.md) file in the main repository. This file is part of the project's public record and is linked from the README, making your contribution visible to the project's tens of thousands of visitors. Contributors who reach core status are additionally highlighted in a dedicated table at the top of that file.
